Latitude vs Maximum Temperature



This graph is showing the impact latitude has on the temperature of a city. Overall, it can be concluded that the lower the city's latitude, the higher its maximum temperature. This indicates that cities closer to the equator have higher maximum temperatures compared to cities located at higher latitudes.
